Smart Study Planning

Active Recall

Testing yourself frequently is 3x more effective than passive reading. Integrate flashcards and practice tests into your daily routine.

Spaced Repetition

Schedule your reviews at increasing intervals. This leverages the "spacing effect" to commit information to long-term memory.

Time Blocking

Dedicate specific chunks of your day to deep work. 90 minutes of focused study beats 4 hours of distracted browsing every time.
